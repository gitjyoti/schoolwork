Abhimaan

Abhimaan is a bollywood movie and bollywood movies are made in Hindi language. In Hindi, abhimaan means pride. The movie was released about 40years ago. It's a story about a popular singer (Amitabh Bachchan) who meets another (Jaya Bhaduri) singer with great potential. He falls in love with her and they get married but her commercial success exceeds his, hurting his pride. As a result, they live separately and struggle until they are reunited.

Main Star Casts:
Amitabh Bachchan
Jaya Badhuri/Bachchan
