FAD symptoms

According to the article published by cnn, following are the symptoms
Lose sleep over Facebook : "If you're staying up late at night because you're on Facebook, and you're tired the next day, Facebook may be a compulsion for you," Lipari said. "You shouldn't be neglecting yourself because of Facebook."
Spend more than an hour a day on Facebook : Pile says it's hard to pinpoint exactly how much is too much time to be spending on social networking. "I can't imagine that anyone would need more than an hour a day on Facebook, and probably no one needs more than 30 minutes," she said.
Become obsessed with old loves : Reconnecting with old friends is one of the great attractions of Facebook, and there's nothing necessarily wrong with "friending" an old boyfriend or girlfriend. But Pile warns that it can get out of hand very quickly.
Ignore work in favor of Facebook "If you're not doing your job in order to sneak time on Facebook, you could have a real problem," Lipari said.
The thought of getting off Facebook leaves you in a cold sweat Pile has her own quick test: "Try going a day without Facebook. If you find it causes you a lot of stress and anxiety, you really need to get some help."


How to defeat Faceboot Addiction

  1. Admit that you have a problem
  2. Write down exactly how much time you spend on Facebook
  3. Give yourself a set time of the day to visit
  4. Turn off email notifications
  5. Meditate as soon as the thought arises
  6. Get off the computer
  7. Write down what you used to do before Facebook
  8. Block the sites from your computer
