My Hobbies


According to Wikipedia :

" A hobby is an activity, interest, enthusiasm, or amateur pastime that is undertaken for pleasure or relaxation, typically done during one's leisure time."

It is interesting to write about my hobbies. When I think of my hobbies, the activities which come in my mind are some new and some old ones. The ones I love to do since I was a child are: sketching and making new plant friends by doing gardening.

I used to love "sketching" and as I grew, I started to I love making portrait till I went to do under-graduation. It is not that I did not get time after that but somehow I could not get time to sit for hours to continue to sketch. I still have a sketch book and my set of pencils.I sometimes think, someday... yes, I will sketch. I will draw something and I have drew once in a while but not to the perfection I used to draw. I used to get so engrossed in sketching that I would sit for hours without realising any hunger, pain or even time.

So, I must say sketching is my hobby because that is what I'd love to do in my leisure time.

However, my another hobby which is "gardening" comparatively takes lesser time. I love it since the time I was taught in school that plants and trees are living beings. Till now whenever I get time I plant one or other thing and spend some of my time taking care of them. I usually plant flowers and from past few years I have been planting herbs, shrubs, vegetables and fruits.
Apart from these ones these days, I do web browsing to learn more on my new found interest "web development".
